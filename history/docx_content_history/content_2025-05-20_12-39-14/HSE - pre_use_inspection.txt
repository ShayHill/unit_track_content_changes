Pre-Use Inspection



Policy



Do not alter tools or create custom tools.



Do not operate machinery or tools with guards in place.



Inspect tools, equipment, and PPE before use.



Confirm that fixed machines are anchored.



Use a face shield when operating bench grinders and hand grinders.



If damage or a defect is found, immediately bring the tool or equipment to the Rig Manager’s house or tag it “out of service”.



Inform the Rig Manager of any defective or damaged tool or equipment.



Do not use defective or damaged tools or equipment.
