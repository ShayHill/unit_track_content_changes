Rotary Table Area



Policy



Use non-slip plates or mats around the rotary table.



Stay clear of the rotary table and rotary mats when drilling.



Proceed directly to the driller’s cabin after ascending the stairs to the rig floor. Do not enter the racking board area without verbal permission or clear consent from the driller or assistant driller.



Inspect slip dies, pins, and handles before each trip.



At least two employees will pull or set slips.



Inspect elevator latches, springs, and pins before each trip.



Do not ride on the elevators.
