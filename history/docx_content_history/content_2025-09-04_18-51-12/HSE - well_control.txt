Well Control



Policy



Drilling Crews will hold at least one well-control drill, per crew, each month. Document these on a tour sheet and, where practicable, KPA on form BOP Drill Form.



Each drilling crew member will have a specific, assigned reporting point in the event of a well-control event.



Inspect blowout prevention equipment daily.



Perform a preventer actuation test each round trip.
