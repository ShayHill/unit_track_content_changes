Cell Phones and Personal Electronic Devices



Policy



Hourly employees and third-party personnel will only use or carry cell phones in the following areas:



--	Designated pre-tour meeting rooms

--	Inside the living quarters

--	Inside the change house

--	In the designated smoking area

--	Inside parked vehicles

--	In yard office spaces



Do not wear earbuds or other listening devices while on duty.
