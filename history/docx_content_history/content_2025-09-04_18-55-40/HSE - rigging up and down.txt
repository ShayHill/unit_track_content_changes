Rigging Up and Down



Policy



These policies affect common rig-up and rig-down operations, but are applicable at all times.



Verify the power is off before connecting or disconnecting electrical lines. (220 and up). This rule applies at all times.



Use tag lines when positioning single-crane loads.



Only qualified personnel will operate Cranes, Cherry Pickers, Winch Trucks, and other lifting or mobile equipment that is not standard on the rig.
