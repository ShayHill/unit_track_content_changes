Safety Meetings



Policy



Unit personnel are required to attend



--	A daily pre-tour safety meeting

--	Pre-job meetings (toolbox talks) at the worksite

--	Periodic company / regional safety meetings



Procedure



Daily Pre-Tour Safety Meeting



1)	Crew leaders or site leadership will

	a)	Set aside approximately 30 minutes for a pre-tour safety meeting.

	b)	Review ongoing operations and safety concerns in a dedicated meeting prior to starting a tour.

	c)	Discuss upcoming operations.

	d)	Review any safety alerts issued or identified by Unit Drilling.

	e)	Identify any newly arrived hazardous chemicals to which personnel may be exposed.

2)	Crewmembers will

	a)	Complete KSA form Job Site Safety Meeting SAF-RIG-5.4 and SAF-RIG-5.4.1.



Pre-Job Safety Meeting



1)	Work crews will

	a)	Create or review a documented, approved <a href="https://unitcorp.kpaehs.com/rsc/67c4bf36d7480a0012d5a2d2">Job Safety Analysis</a>.

	b)	Identify and control site-specific, condition-specific, and personnel-specific hazards as required by <a href="https://unitcorp.kpaehs.com/rsc/67c4bf36d7480a0012d5a2d2">Job Safety Analysis</a>.
