CPR and First Aid Preparedness



Policy



CPR Training



Two personnel at Assistant Driller or higher level will be present and trained to perform Frist Aid, including CPR, at Unit Drilling rigs and yards.



Unit Drilling will offer periodic CPR training to all personnel as needed to fulfill this requirement.



CPR training expires every two years.



Training



Unit Personnel expected to provide first aid will be trained per 29 CRF 1910.1030(g)(2)(vii) before first assignment and then annually.<a href="https://www.osha.gov/laws-regs/regulations/standardnumber/1910/1910.1030"></a>



First-Aid Supplies



Unit Drilling rigs and facilities will maintain



--	a bloodborne pathogens kit per <a href="https://unitcorp.kpaehs.com/rsc/67c4bf36d7480a0012d5a2c6">Bloodborne Pathogen Exposure Control</a> in the Rig Manager’s or Facility Manager’s office

--	an ANSI/ISEA Z308.1 compliant, type 1, class B first aid kit in the Rig Manager’s or Facility Manager’s office

--	eyewash bottles or stations available for immediate use where splash or eye-contaminant injuries are foreseeable



Unit Drilling rigs will maintain eyewash stations or eyewash bottles



--	in the Rig Manager’s or Facility Manager’s office

--	in the drill cabin

--	in the mud house

--	at the third-party pre-mix pit



Unit Drilling rigs will maintain a chemical shower on the mud pits.
