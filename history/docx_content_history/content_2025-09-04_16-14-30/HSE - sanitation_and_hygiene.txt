Sanitation and Hygiene



Policy



Keep campsite clean, swept, and mopped.



Do not use refillable shared water coolers or cans. Water dispensers with single-use water jugs are permitted.



The rig manager will perform a monthly crew quarters inspection using form Rig Inspection Health, Safety and Environmental Management System SAF-RIG.
