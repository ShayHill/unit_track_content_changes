Pressure Testing



Policy



Establish a 50-foot buffer zone around pressure testing.



While pressure testing the BOP stack:



--	Do not enter the substructure.

--	Stay 50 feet from the choke and kill lines or any other equipment being tested.

--	Stay 50 feet from any pressurized hozes.

--	Do not approach the rotary table.
