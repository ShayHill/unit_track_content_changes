Testing Live Equipment



Policy



Unit Drilling personnel will not perform maintenance on live electrical equipment. Unit personnel may perform electrical tests on some live electrical equipment as below.



Main Generators

Third-party company

Breakers

Qualified Unit Drilling personnel

Top Drive Motor

Third-party company

Drawworks Motor

Third-party company

Mud Pump Motor

Third-party company

Busbars

Third-party company (480 and above) 

Agitator and Mixing Motors

Qualified Unit Drilling personnel

Other Equipment over 480 volts

Third-party company



Do not open or test live electrical equipment without



--	A non-conductive mat

--	Non-conductive gloves
