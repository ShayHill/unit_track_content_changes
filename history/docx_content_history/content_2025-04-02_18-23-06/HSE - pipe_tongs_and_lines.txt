Pipe Tongs and Lines



Policy



Do not install makeshift weights to adjust the counterbalance of rig tongs.



Wear eye protection and a face shield when tapping dies in our out.



Do not use the rotary table to break pipe connections. Use the iron roughneck or two sets of rig tongs. Exception: Use the rotary table and one set of tongs to break the bit.



Move personnel away from the floor before using unusual force to break connection.



Move personnel out of the backside radius of the tongs (the area between the tongs and the draw works) before torquing a pipe joint.
