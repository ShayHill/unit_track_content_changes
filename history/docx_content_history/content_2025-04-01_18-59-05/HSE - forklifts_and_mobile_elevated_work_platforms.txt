Forklifts and Mobile Elevated Work Platforms (MEWPs)



Policy



Before operating a forklift or MEWP:



--	Complete a documented Basic Operator Safety Training course. This course will meet the requirements of <a href="https://www.osha.gov/laws-regs/regulations/standardnumber/1910/1910.178">29 CFR 1910.178(l)(3)</a>.

--	Complete a forklift evaluation using KPA form Performance Evaluation – Forklift or Performance Evaluation – Aerial & Scissor Lift.



Do not operate a forklift or MEWP unless you are aware of the maximum lifting capacity.



Do not step more than 10 feet away from a forklift or MEWP while it is running.



Perform a documented daily inspection using KPA form Forklift and Aerial Lift Inspection on any day a forklift or MEWP is used. Tag out the forklift or MEWP and do not operate it if any deficiencies are found.



Do not use the forklift to lift or carry personnel or carry passengers in the forklift seat.



Do not park a forklift with the forks elevated.



Do not move a Mobile Elevated Work Platform with the boom extended.



Personnel will be scheduled for re-training after any incident with a forklift or Mobile Elevated Work Platform. See <a href="https://unitcorp.kpaehs.com/rsc/67c4bf36d7480a0012d5a2ce">Incident Investigation</a> for clarification on the word “incident”.
