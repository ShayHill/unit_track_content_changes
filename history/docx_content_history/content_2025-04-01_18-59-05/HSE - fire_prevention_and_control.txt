Fire Prevention and Control



Policy



Extinguisher Locations



Unit and Unit contractors will provide portable fire extinguishers, mounted and identified for easy access, within 50 feet of any potential fire hazard.



Unit will not use cartridge-operated fire extinguishers.



Additional required extinguishers



--	For rigs, one portable fire extinguisher at each point marked on the <a href="https://unitcorp.kpaehs.com/rsc/67c4bf36d7480a0012d5a2ca">Fire Prevention and Control – Extinguisher Map</a>.

--	One dry-chemical extinguisher in each company owned vehicle.

--	One dry-chemical extinguisher in each forklift, mobile-work-platform, or similar powered, mobile equipment.



Prevention



--	Designate and mark a smoking area for each rig.

--	Keep explosion-proof covers in place when not servicing equipment.

--	Do not allow flammable materials to accumulate beneath engine skids, in the substructure, or similar areas.

--	Do not refuel an engine while it is running or hot.

--	Follow additional guidelines in <a href="https://unitcorp.kpaehs.com/rsc/67c4bf36d7480a0012d5a2de">Housekeeping</a>.



Response



Unit rig personnel will be trained to respond to incipient fires.



Inspection



Perform a documented fire-extinguisher inspection monthly using KPA Monthly Fire Extinguisher Inspection SAF-RIG-3.7 or SAF-SHOP-3.7.



Use



After using a fire extinguisher,



--	Place an unused fire extinguisher in the location from which the fire extinguisher was taken.

--	Recharge the used fire extinguisher before mounting it again.
