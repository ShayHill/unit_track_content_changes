General Fleet Safety



Policy



These policies apply when operating a company vehicle or operating a personal vehicle on company time or for company business.



--	Follow all requirements in Corp – Automobile Usage on KPA.

--	Follow all requirements in UDC – Vehicle Usage Policy on KPA.

--	Shut off the engine while refueling.

--	Do not carry flammable liquids. Exception: you may carry sample containers handled by gas testers. Keep these in sealed containers.

--	Remove keys and lock vehicle when parking.

--	Inspect vehicle using KPA form <a href="https://unitcorp.kpaehs.com/forms/responses/new/230667">Fleet Vehicle Inspection</a>.



Complete and submit a drug screen before operating a company vehicle.
